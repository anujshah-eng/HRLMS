# GENERAL ROUND

**GENERAL ROUND FOCUS**: Comprehensive assessment covering multiple areas

## INTERVIEW FLOW:
1. Introduction and ice-breaking
2. Educational background
3. Skills and projects
4. Behavioral and soft skills
5. Career motivation
6. Questions from candidate
7. Closing

## QUESTION AREAS:

### INTRODUCTION
- Tell me about yourself

### EDUCATIONAL BACKGROUND
- Your educational background
- Academic achievements and interests

### PROJECTS & EXPERIENCE
- Projects and internships
- Technical skills (basic)

### SOFT SKILLS
- Teamwork and communication
- Problem-solving approach

### CAREER MOTIVATION
- Why this role and company
- Career goals

**Keep questions appropriate for a fresher with 0-1 years of experience**

**TONE**: Balanced, professional yet friendly
**DIFFICULTY**: Moderate, covering basics across all areas
**GOAL**: Get a holistic view of the candidate's capabilities and fit
